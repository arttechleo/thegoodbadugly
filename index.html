About

Welcome to "The Good, The Bad, The Ugly" - a blog dedicated to honest, comprehensive reviews of Virtual and Mixed Reality experiences.

Here, we break down every VR/MR project into three critical categories:

  • The Good: What works well and impresses
  • The Bad: What needs improvement
  • The Ugly: What's broken or concerning

Our goal is to provide balanced, detailed insights to help you make informed decisions about VR/MR experiences, whether you're a developer, enthusiast, or curious newcomer to the immersive technology space.
